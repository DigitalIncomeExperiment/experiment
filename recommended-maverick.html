The Maverick's Adventures series is published through Amazon KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing) — Amazon's free self-publishing platform. It costs nothing to publish. Royalties of up to 70% on ebooks and around 60% on paperbacks (after print costs) are available. Experiment 01 is testing whether consistent publishing can build meaningful passive income over time.
