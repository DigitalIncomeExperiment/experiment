What This Site Is

The Digital Income Experiment is an independent personal project. It documents the real experience of one private individual testing nine online income methods simultaneously. All income figures reported are genuine and verified before publication.

The site exists to answer an honest question with honest data — nothing more. There is no product being sold, no course, no coaching programme, and no paid membership.

Content is produced and managed by Digital Revenue Studio, who act as content creators for this project. DRS do not own or operate the Experiment and do not direct its findings.

02

Results Are Personal and Not Typical

Every income figure published on this site represents the real earnings of one specific person, in specific circumstances, over a specific period of time. They are not projections, guarantees, or typical results.

Online income is highly variable and depends on factors including time invested, existing skills, audience size, location, market conditions, and many other variables specific to each individual. What one person earns from a platform or method may bear no relation to what another person earns.

Do not use the figures on this site as a basis for financial decisions. They are provided as honest data points from one experiment, not as benchmarks you can expect to replicate.
