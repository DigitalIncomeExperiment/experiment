Experiment 05 — first real comparison result

First head-to-head test complete. A new hoover purchased through Currys — Quidco offered 3.75% floorcare cashback, TopCashback offered 5.75%. TopCashback won on rate. Tracked immediately at £1.65 on a tracked value of £158.33 (merchants often track at a lower rate and uplift to the correct value on verification). Estimated payout: 9 weeks.
