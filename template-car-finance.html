The 3 DCA Types

Negative DCA
Rate was increased to earn higher commission. You were financially disadvantaged. Redress possible.
Neutral DCA
DCA present but not used to change your rate. No financial disadvantage. Unlikely to result in compensation.
Positive DCA
Used to lower your rate. You benefited. Unlikely to result in compensation.

Checklist

  • Agreement must be before 28 Jan 2021
  • Send to your lender directly — not a claims company
  • Send by email — keep the timestamped record
  • Note whether confirmed DCA is negative, neutral or positive
  • FCA redress scheme estimated end May 2026
  • If no response, follow up after 8 weeks
