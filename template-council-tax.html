Before You Send

  • Do the neighbours check first — find 5+ comparables on gov.uk/council-tax-bands
  • Check your 1991 estimated value using the Nationwide HPI tool
  • Only challenge if evidence is clear and consistent — a challenge can push neighbours up too
  • Keep a timestamped copy of your submission
  • Be patient — this process can take 2+ years
  • If successful, ask for a cash refund rather than a credit
